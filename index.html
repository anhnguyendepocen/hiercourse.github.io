Program

See below for the program of the R course. The Friday PM session will consist of a choice of topics.

The AM session runs from 9:30-12:30, the PM session from 13:30-16:30.

Monday AM Basics of R (1) (Optional) (Chapter 1)
PM Reading and subsetting data (Chapter 2)
Tuesday AM Special data types (Chapter 3)
PM Visualizing data (Chapter 4)
Wednesday AM Basic statistics & External resources (Chapter 5 and 13)
PM Summarizing and tabulating data (Chapter 6)
Thursday AM Linear modelling (Chapter 7)
PM Multivariate statistics & Mixed-effects models (Chapter 8 and extra)
Friday AM Programming : functions, lists and loops (Chapter 9)
PM Project management (Chapter 10) and Choice of topics (TBA)

About Us

Remko Duursma and Jeff Powell are at the Hawkesbury Institute for the Environment at Western Sydney University.

Remko Duursma

Remko's research interests centre on understanding vegetation structure and function with models and data, including water use, carbon uptake, canopy structure, and radiation absorption of trees and forests. Visit his website here.

Jeff Powell

Jeff's research interests centre on understanding community assembly processes, particularly in the context of soil ecology and plant-microbe interactions, and relating these to ecosystem function.
