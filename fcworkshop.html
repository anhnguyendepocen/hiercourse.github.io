Introduction to R: 3-day workshop

This page accompanies the workshop 'Data analysis and visualization with R', held on 5-7 February 2018. See below for learning materials (manual as PDF, and additional handouts), the Solutions to exercises, and a link to the page with the datasets used throughout the manual.

Program

Each day consists of a morning session (9:30 - 12:30), and afternoon session (13:30 - 16:30). Each session starts with a lecture and demonstration (45 - 60min), followed by ample time for practice. An exception is the Monday AM session, which includes two demonstration sessions.

Since this workshop is for a small number of attendees, the program is very flexible. Topics can be switched, compressed or expanded at will.

Day Time Topic Chapter
Monday 9:30 - 9:45 Welcome, Opening R + Rstudio 1
9:45 - 12:30 Basic operations, vectors, dataframes (demonstration and practice) 1,2
13:30 - 14:30 Subsetting data, special datatypes (demonstration) 2,3
14:30 - 16:30 Hands-on practice 2,3
Tuesday 9:30 - 10:30 Visualizing data (demonstration) 4
10:30 - 12:30 Hands-on practice 4
13:30 - 14:45 Basic statistics and linear modelling (demonstration) 5,7
14:45 - 16:30 Hands-on practice 5,7
Wednesday 9:30 - 10:15 Summarizing, tabulating and merging data (demonstration) 6
10:15 - 12:30 Hands-on practice 6
12:30 - 13:15 Linear Mixed-Effects Models; other topics 6
13:15 - 16:30 Hands-on practice 6
