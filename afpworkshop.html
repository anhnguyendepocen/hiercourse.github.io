Introduction to R: 2-day workshop

This page accompanies the workshop 'Data analysis and visualization with R', held on 29-30 May 2017. See below for learning materials (manual as PDF, and additional handouts), the Solutions to exercises, and a link to the page with the datasets used throughout the manual.

Location and parking

The workshop is held on the Hawkesbury Campus of Western Sydney University. Follow this link for a map of the campus and on-site parking information ($8 for a day permit). Note that the location of parking ticket machines is incorrectly shown on the map - they have been relocated to a central location on each main parking lot. For the workshop, it is best to park on P14 (ample parking will be available).

The venue is building G6, room 1.12 (computer lab). Follow the link above for a map of the campus.
